# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Education AI founders, product leaders, eval leaders, and hiring teams who need proof that Josh can identify and measure AI education failures before a district pilot or production release exposes them.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio makes Josh's education-domain evaluation work legible and credible. It should convert serious visitors toward the work archive, the fixed-fee evaluation sprint, or direct contact by showing runnable proof, not generic positioning.

## Brand Personality

Precise, empirical, domain-fluent, and quietly premium. The site should feel like a diagnostic instrument run by someone who has been in both the classroom and the evaluation harness.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dark AI portfolios, GitHub README styling, startup gradient gloss, typewriter gimmicks, glass panels for decoration, and overbuilt motion that competes with the case studies.

## Design Principles

- Make the site itself evidence of technical taste.
- Lead with the education-eval wedge, then expose the runnable proof quickly.
- Use motion to clarify attention, not to perform.
- Keep the stack durable enough for nightly regeneration.
- Preserve trust through restrained, accessible interaction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, 44px touch targets for controls, semantic links and buttons, and a complete reduced-motion path for all animation.
